ArchAlign Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2026

ArchAlign respects your privacy. This app is designed to process photos directly on your device and does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

Photo Access

ArchAlign may request access to your photo library so you can select images for perspective correction and save edited images back to your photo library.

Photos selected by the user are processed locally on the device. ArchAlign does not upload your photos to any server.

Camera Access

ArchAlign may request camera access so you can capture architectural photos directly in the app.

Camera images are processed locally on the device. ArchAlign does not record, upload, or transmit camera content.

Photo Metadata

When saving edited photos, ArchAlign may preserve original photo metadata when possible, such as capture date, camera information, lens information, and location metadata already embedded in the original photo.

ArchAlign does not independently collect your location.

Data Collection

ArchAlign does not collect any personal data.

The app does not use analytics, advertising trackers, third-party tracking SDKs, account login systems, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

Data Sharing

ArchAlign does not share user data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

ArchAlign does not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact jwlise@gmail.com.

This policy applies to the ArchAlign iOS app.
